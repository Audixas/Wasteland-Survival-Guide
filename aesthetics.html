Wasteland Survival Guide

Aesthetics

First, create a "Aesthetics" separator in MO2.

As you install the mods from this section, organize them underneath this separator.
If you are curious/disappointed by the lack of texture mods in this section, the WSG team doesn't really use texture mods & they are generally compatible with everything, so for that reason they are considered outside of the scope of the guide. If you are interested in texture packs, here is a list contributed by Salamand3r to help you get started. No support is provided for these textures, but do enjoy.
