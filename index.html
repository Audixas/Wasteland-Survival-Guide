A Tale of Two Wastelands 3.3 Modding Guide

Tale of Two Wastelands is a total conversion project that seamlessly merges Fallout 3 and its DLC into Fallout: New Vegas, allowing both games to be played in a single playthrough. It upgrades Fallout 3's mechanics to those of FNV without compromising the feel and balance of the original. New Vegas mechanics like companion wheel, companion perks, weapon mods, crafting, recipes, harvestable plants, and poisons have been added to the DC wasteland items, NPCs, and world.

The following guide will walk you through installing and customizing some of the most essential mods to enrich your experience with the game! Patches and settings will be provided where necessary as well as a load oBrder to ensure a smooth modding experience for users of any level of experience.

This guide uses Mod Organizer 2, which will hereafter be referred to as MO2. No former experience is required with the program, however:
We regrettably are unable to provide instructions or support for any other mod managers, including but not limited to: Vortex, FOMM, NMM, and Wyre.
Please do not attempt to follow this guide on any other mod manager, as the instructions given specifically pertain to MO2, and no support will be possible.

Requirements - You NEED all of these before continuing!

  • English copies of Fallout 3 and New Vegas, with all DLCs, on either Steam or GOG.
    • GOTY/Ultimate edition is not a factor, as long as you have all of the DLCs for both games.
    • You may "mix and match" Steam and GOG versions; e.g. FO3 on GOG and FNV on Steam is supported.
    • Bethesda.net and Game Pass versions are not supported; nor will they ever due to fundamental incompatibility with NVSE.
  • About 30 GB of space free for installation
  • Windows 7 or higher (64bit)
  • DirectX Runtime Libraries
  • Microsoft VC++ 2013 and Microsoft VC++ 2015-2019 (x86 AND x64 versions)
    • You need both the x86 AND x64 versions of the above libraries, even if you have a 64-bit system.
  • 7-Zip
  • The latest GPU drivers (Either NVIDIA or AMD).
  • A Nexus Mods account
